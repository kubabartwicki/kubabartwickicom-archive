Take Me Home

Take Me Home is a bike rack that questions forms in interior design. Unlike all else in your room, it can serve only one practical function and when it cannot, it becomes a sculpture. Take Me Home focuses on form and function, purpose and aesthetics.

Take Me Home has been designed in London and made in Gdansk, Poland. It is a response to a self-directed brief at Camberwell College of Arts, and is a result of cooperation with my dad and brother, both experienced in furniture making.

Take Me Home comes with a set of instructions and equipment on how to attach to the wall. The process is simple and aims to bring you closer to your furniture.

The photographs purposefully do not show the product in its intended setting. Each space Take Me Home ends up in is unique, and we encourage creativity in its placement.
