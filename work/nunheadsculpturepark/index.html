This is “Nunhead Sculpture Park”

    :ghost:

  • A project exposing the confused nature of a modern-day cemetery. Created as a response to the site of Nunhead Cemetery, one of the oldest and largest graveyards in London.

    The result represents the space as a sculpture park; highlighting the long-lost purpose of burying the dead, as well as the eerie atmosphere of anonymous, unmarked graves.

    The outcome comes in two forms: a book and a series of prints.

  • The prints are a close-up, a closer look at one of the pieces in the Nunhead Sculpture Park.
